Engineering Device Usage Policy
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 2026-01-12
Owner: Engineering Operations

Purpose
To govern the usage of laptops, development machines, and test devices.

Scope
Applies to all engineers and contractors using company-provided devices.

1. Permitted Use
Devices are provided for business use. Limited personal use is allowed if it does not interfere with security or productivity.

2. Administrative Rights
Engineering laptops may have elevated privileges, but users must not disable security software.
