Meal Allowance Policy
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 2026-01-10
Owner: Finance - Travel Desk

Purpose
To specify daily meal allowances during business travel.

Scope
Applies to domestic and international travel where meals are not already provided by the hotel or event.

1. Domestic Travel
Meal expenses are reimbursable up to 800 INR per day, excluding alcohol.

2. International Travel
Meal limits are defined by destination and published annually by Finance.

3. Receipts
Itemised receipts are required for all meal claims.
