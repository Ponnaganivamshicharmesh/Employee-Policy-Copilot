Procurement Policy
Version: 1.1
Effective Date: 2026-02-20
Owner: Procurement

Purpose
To ensure fair, transparent, and cost-effective procurement of goods and services.

Scope
Applies to all purchases above 10,000 INR.

1. Vendor Selection
At least two competitive quotes are required for purchases between 10,000 and 100,000 INR.

2. Purchase Orders
A valid purchase order must be approved before committing to any vendor.
