Travel Reimbursement Policy
Version: 1.2
Effective Date: 2026-01-10
Owner: Finance - Travel Desk

Purpose
To define reimbursable business travel expenses and the process for claiming them.

Scope
Applies to employees who travel on approved company business.

1. Eligible Expenses
Eligible expenses for reimbursement include economy airfare, train tickets, local transportation, hotel accommodation, and meals, all within approved daily limits.

2. Meal Allowance
Yes, meal expenses are reimbursable, capped at 800 INR per day for domestic travel, unless pre-approved for a higher limit.

3. Pre-Approvals
All travel must be approved in advance by the reporting manager and, where applicable, the budget owner.

4. Claim Submission
Claims must be submitted within 15 calendar days of trip completion through the expense system with itemised receipts.

Exceptions
Exceptions require written approval from the department head and Finance.

FAQs
Q: Are ride-sharing services reimbursable?
A: Yes, when used for local travel during an approved trip and supported by receipts.
