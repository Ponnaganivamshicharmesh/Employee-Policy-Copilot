General Expense Policy
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 2026-01-05
Owner: Corporate Finance

Purpose
To define reimbursable business expenses and ensure prudent use of company funds.

Scope
Applies to all employees incurring expenses on behalf of the company.

1. Reasonable and Necessary
Expenses must be reasonable, necessary for business purposes, and properly documented.

2. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
Personal entertainment, family travel, traffic fines, and luxury upgrades are not reimbursable.

3. Approval Workflow
All expenses require line manager approval; expenses above defined thresholds may require additional finance approval.
