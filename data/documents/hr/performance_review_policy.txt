Performance Review Policy
Version: 2.0
Effective Date: 2026-03-01
Owner: Talent Management

Purpose
To define the framework for fair and consistent performance evaluations.

Scope
Covers all full-time employees who have completed at least 6 months of service.

1. Review Cycle
Formal performance reviews occur twice a year: mid-year and year-end.

2. Ratings
Managers assign ratings based on goal achievement, competencies, and values.

3. Calibration
HR facilitates calibration sessions to ensure rating consistency across teams.

FAQs
Q: Can I appeal my rating?
A: Yes, you may request a review meeting with your manager and HR if you believe your rating does not reflect your performance.
