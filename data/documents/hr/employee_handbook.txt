Employee Handbook
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 2026-01-01
Owner: People Operations

Purpose
The Employee Handbook provides a concise overview of the company's core expectations, workplace standards, and available support programs.

Scope
This handbook applies to all full-time, part-time, contract, and internship employees globally.

1. Work Culture and Values
The company expects employees to act with integrity, respect colleagues, and protect confidential information.

2. Work Hours
Standard work hours are 9:00 to 18:00 local time, Monday to Friday. Flexible arrangements must be approved in writing by the reporting manager.

3. Workplace Conduct
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are strictly prohibited. Violations may lead to disciplinary action up to and including termination.

4. Information Security
Employees must use only company-approved devices and follow IT security policies.

FAQs
Q: Is remote work allowed?
A: Remote work is allowed based on team policy and manager approval.

Q: Where can I find detailed policies?
A: Detailed policies are available in the HR, Finance, IT, and Compliance policy documents.
