Leave Policy
Version: 1.1
Effective Date: 2026-02-01
Owner: HR Operations

Purpose
To define the process for requesting, approving, and tracking employee leave.

Scope
Applies to all full-time employees. Intern and contractor leave is governed by contract terms.

1. Annual Leave
Employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.

2. Leave Approval Process
All planned leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by the reporting manager before the leave start date.
Unplanned emergency leave should be notified to the manager as soon as reasonably possible.

3. Carry-Forward and Encashment
A maximum of 5 days of annual leave can be carried forward to the next calendar year. Encashment is not permitted except where mandated by local law.

4. Unauthorised Absence
Absence without approval may be treated as leave without pay and can trigger a performance discussion.

FAQs
Q: Can I cancel approved leave?
A: Yes, you can cancel or modify approved leave in the HR portal, subject to manager approval.

Q: Are public holidays included in annual leave?
A: Public holidays are separate and do not reduce annual leave balance.
