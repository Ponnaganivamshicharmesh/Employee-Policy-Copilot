Attendance and Punctuality Policy
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 2026-01-15
Owner: HR Business Partners

Purpose
To set expectations for employee attendance and punctuality.

Scope
Applies to all employees, including those on hybrid or remote work arrangements.

1. Recording Attendance
Employees must record attendance daily in the HR or time-tracking system.

2. Late Arrival
Arrivals more than 15 minutes after scheduled start time are considered late unless pre-approved.

3. Repeated Non-Compliance
More than 3 late arrivals in a month may lead to a formal discussion and, if continued, corrective action.

FAQs
Q: Does this apply to flexible schedules?
A: Yes, but the agreed flexible schedule becomes the reference for punctuality.
